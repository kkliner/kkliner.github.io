Background

I'm originally from a small gold rush town in Northern California but I moved to LA a few years ago for work. I went to school for electrical engineering, so website design is not my wheelhouse. I intend on using this as a platform to record some of my more interesting personal projects that I work on whenever I have time and maybe as a blog for some of my thoughts.

Inspiration

I'm started this website to learn about about website development on my free time. Currently, the entire website is in raw HTML, paritally becuase I have no idea what I'm doing and partially for fun. It makes the website super compatible and very quick to load. Open it in an ancient version of Internet Explorer, I dare you.

The inspirations for the minimal design is txti and this satirical website. Both are entirely text based, and while I don't plan on restricting this website to not include any photos, I do think that many modern websites could do with far fewer images and still get their point across.

The URL is my amateur radio callsign, if anyone was wondering.
